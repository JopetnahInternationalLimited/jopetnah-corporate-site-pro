Company Overview

About Jopetnah International Limited

A diversified international conglomerate and social enterprise, headquartered in Kampala, Uganda, delivering integrated solutions across 16 strategic sectors.

Jopetnah International Limited is a diversified international conglomerate and social enterprise committed to delivering innovative, integrated, and sustainable solutions across sixteen strategic sectors.

Established in 2016 and headquartered in Kampala, Uganda, the company was founded on the belief that today's complex challenges require multidisciplinary expertise, strategic partnerships, and a relentless commitment to excellence.

Our integrated business model enables governments, private enterprises, development partners, investors, educational institutions, NGOs, and communities to access a comprehensive portfolio of professional services through one trusted corporate partner.

Driven by innovation, operational excellence, and responsible business practices, we transform ideas into measurable impact, helping organizations improve efficiency, unlock growth opportunities, strengthen resilience, and create sustainable value.

How We Started

Our Story

Jopetnah International Limited was established with the ambition of building more than a company—it was created to build an institution capable of solving complex business and development challenges through integrated expertise.

What began as a consultancy-driven enterprise has evolved into a diversified international organization serving clients across multiple industries and geographical markets.

Today, our multidisciplinary capabilities span strategic advisory, engineering, technology, healthcare, logistics, agriculture, energy, education, tourism, international recruitment, marketing, mining, events management, research and development, FMCG, and international trade.

By bringing these complementary sectors together under one corporate identity, we create synergies that improve project delivery, reduce complexity for our clients, and generate long-term economic and social value.

Our Vision

To be a globally respected leader in integrated business solutions, recognized for empowering businesses, strengthening institutions, fostering innovation, and advancing sustainable development across Africa and the international marketplace.

Our Mission

To empower businesses, governments, development partners, and communities by delivering innovative, sustainable, and results-driven solutions across diverse industries. We achieve this by combining strategic thinking, technical excellence, responsible leadership, and collaborative partnerships that create lasting value for our stakeholders while contributing to inclusive economic growth and sustainable development.
